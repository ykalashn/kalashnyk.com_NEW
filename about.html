About me

My journey

Born and raised in rural Ukraine, I grew up surrounded by cherry trees and community. Today, I'm based in Helsinki, pursuing a master's degree in marketing at Aalto University. My path from a small village to one of Europe's leading innovation hubs has been filled with diverse experiences — from volunteering in kindergartens and organising events to designing user interfaces and pitching at international competitions.

Along the way I've learned to bridge cultures and disciplines. I taught English and robotics to preschoolers in Estonia, built human-friendly cybersecurity tools, gamified elevator rides for KONE, and helped improve hearing health through storytelling. These experiences taught me empathy, adaptability and the power of simple narratives.

As a vegan and member of the LGBTQ+ community, I care deeply about inclusion and sustainability. I'm currently applying for Finnish citizenship and sharpening my Finnish — slowly but surely. When I'm not studying, you'll find me exploring Helsinki's coffee shops, hosting community events or events like Slush or Junction. I'm also learning to play piano!


Values & interests

  • Empathy & accessibility: I strive to create products that everyone can use, regardless of age, ability or background.
  • Storytelling: Great ideas deserve great stories. I love turning complex problems into clear, engaging narratives.
  • Community: From organising student events to volunteering, I thrive when bringing people together.
  • Lifelong learning: Whether mastering Finnish or learning the latest in service research, I never stop exploring.
