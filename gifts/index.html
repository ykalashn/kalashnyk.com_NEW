about gifts

I like gifts. I do not want you to feel pressure to buy one.

A gift is a consumption choice. It should create value for me without creating waste for you. When in doubt, no gift is a better choice.

quick facts

I'm vegan. Please avoid leather, wool, silk, beeswax and animal-tested items.

Used / second-hand is great.
