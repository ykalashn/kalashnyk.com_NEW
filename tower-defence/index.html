The Vision

Players can invent their own theme and characters with AI by prompting, uploading a photo or drawing, then pick priorities (strength vs speed vs support, etc.). The AI proposes abilities that match the vibe. Players can generate entire worlds with their own rule sets. This can include, for example, different physics, progression models, enemy logic, resource systems and status effects. Each world extends the base game instead of simply reskinning it, so the same core platform can produce completely different game themes with genuinely different metas and strategies in the same tower defence framework.

At scale, the platform runs automated simulations on every new world ruleset. It flags broken interactions and the AI suggests ways to balance the world, so players get to stay creative but the game stays fair and playable. Portals to worlds can be shared, traded or featured in curated playlists where only certified content is allowed.

If this works, it pushes UGC from 'make a map/skin' to 'create mechanics that rewrite the meta' without requiring technical skills. Roblox and Fortnite proved the demand for creator ecosystems. This is the next step: a platform where non-coders can ship genuinely new gameplay and where quality and fairness scale through automated governance rather than manual policing.
